Pros

  • More length can reach more areas in larger streams
  • The more expensive poles are more sturdy!
  • The kits come with everything you need to begin.

Cons

  • More length is worse for smaller streams
  • The more expensive poles burn a hole in your wallet
  • The flies tend to be very weak and often times you have to buy other ones.
